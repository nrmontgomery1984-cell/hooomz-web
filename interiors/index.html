How it works

No surprises.
No runaround.
Just the work.

We built a system before we took our first job. Every project follows the same process — so you always know where things stand, what's coming next, and exactly what you're paying for.

01
Site Visit — we come to you
Laser measurements, LiDAR scan of the space, and a proper look at what the subfloor and substrate are telling us. Free of charge, no obligation.
02
Good / Better / Best quote
Three material tiers, one scope of work. You pick the product level — the labour and process don't change. No hidden surprises when you choose the middle option.
03
SCRIPT — our production method
Every install follows our stage-gated workflow: substrate check, layout, cut list, install, punch, turnover. No skipped steps, no "we'll get to that later."
04
Turnover — your home, better than you left it
Photo documentation, warranty package, and a clean site. We don't leave until it's done. You'll know because you'll have the photos to prove it.

The SCRIPT method

Every job. Every time.

SCRIPT is our production methodology. Six stages, no skipped steps. You'll always know which stage your project is in.

S
Shield
Protect surfaces, contain the work area, and confirm substrate is ready
C
Clear
Remove existing material and address any subfloor or substrate issues
R
Ready
Layout lines, cut list confirmed, material staged and acclimated
I
Install
The main event — executed to the cut list, no improvisation
P
Punch
Walk the job, document every item, close every open issue before leaving
T
Turnover
Photos, warranty package, clean site. Your home back — better than we found it
